A stunning Balinese villa perched above the Indian Ocean with breathtaking panoramic views. Designed by a renowned local architect, the property seamlessly blends traditional Balinese craftsmanship with contemporary luxury.

The villa features a private infinity pool that appears to merge with the ocean horizon, an open-air living pavilion adorned with local timber and stone, and four spacious bedroom suites each with en-suite bathrooms and ocean-facing balconies.

Services include a personal butler, private chef for breakfast, daily housekeeping, airport transfers, and in-villa spa treatments. The property sits on a cliff with direct access to a secluded beach below via a private pathway. Surrounded by tropical gardens maintained by a dedicated groundskeeper, this is the ultimate private retreat in Bali.
